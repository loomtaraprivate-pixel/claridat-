Common Questions

Questions Defense Contractors Ask

Does Claridat replace my compliance consultant? ▼
Claridat handles the documentation, tracking, and process work that consumes 90% of consultant time. For formal CMMC C3PAO audits you'll still need a professional — but far less often, because you've already done the groundwork.
What CMMC levels does Claridat cover? ▼
Level 1 and Level 2, which apply to 95% of defense subcontractors handling FCI and CUI. Level 3 requires government-led assessments outside any software scope.
How accurate are AI-generated documents? ▼
Claridat generates structured drafts based on NIST SP 800-171 and current CMMC assessment guides. Every document requires your review before use. You start from an 80% draft instead of a blank page — saving 10–20 hours each.
What happens to my data? ▼
Encrypted at rest and in transit. We never train on your data, never share it, and you can export or delete everything at any time.
When does Claridat launch? ▼
Q2 2026 for founding members. Pre-order now to lock permanent pricing and get priority onboarding.
Can a prime contractor use Claridat for their supply chain? ▼
Yes — the Prime tier lets you manage multiple subcontractors, track their compliance status, and auto-generate flow-down clause documentation.
